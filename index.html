About Me

Weather App

SUMMARY

This Weather App allows the user to search for a city and upon submission, receive the current weather information for that city. That includes the temperature, the humidity, the wind speed and the UV Index. They are also presented with a 5 day forecast that informs of the temperature and humidity for the next 5 days. Upon searching a city, that city is saved and added to the page. If the user presses that city name again, the weather dashboard will once again display the information for that city.

Upstaged

SUMMARY

Upstaged was created by theatre lovers, for theatre lovers. Upstaged is a multi-faceted and highly entertaining theatre app, created to entertain, amuse, and enjoy. There are various games, quizzes, videos, and social feeds. Theatre Jeopardy, Hamilton Trivia, and a Playbill creator, just to name a few of the interesting features on the site. You can stay up to date on what's trending in broadway with our News page, as well as our Show-Score, Twitter, and Youtube feeds. With every game or quiz, you have a chance to gain points and rise through the rank. Stay tuned for a chat feature, so you can enjoy all of this in live time with friends, competitors and fellow theatre lovers.
